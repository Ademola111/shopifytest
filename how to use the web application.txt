HOW TO USE KINSHA LOGISTIC
1. click on create inventory on the top right corner to create an inventory
2. click on add product of add various items to each inventory
3. click on view product to see product details
4. click on edit product to change product details. Note you can't update the product image only without filling all field
    like price, description and size the title can be left on change.
5. You can delete an item from the list of product that will be displayed from the database. once you click on delete
    you will be notify to confirm your action or cancle if is by mistake to retain the product been showcased
6. You can view each products per inventory category with the use of inventory menu tab.
